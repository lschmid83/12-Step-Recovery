Eighth Step Prayer

Higher Power, I ask Your help in making my list of all those I have harmed.
I will take responsibility for my mistakes and Be forgiving to others as You are forgiving to me.
Grant me the willingness to begin my restitution.
This I pray.

"We attempt to sweep away the debris which has accumulated out of our effort to live on self-will and run the show ourselves.
If we haven't the will to do this, we ask until it comes."

God help me to become willing to sweep away the debris of self will and self-reliant living. Thy will be done for his person as well as for me.
Amen.
