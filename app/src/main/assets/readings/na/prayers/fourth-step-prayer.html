Fourth Step Prayer

Dear God, It is I who has made my life a mess. I have done it, but I cannot undo it.
My mistakes are mine and I will begin a searching and fearless moral inventory.
I will write down my wrongs. But I will also include that which is good.
I pray for the strength to complete the task.
Amen.

When in Doubt

"I am to sit quietly when in doubt, asking only for direction and strength to meet my problems as He would have me.
Never am I to pray for myself, except as my requests bore on my usefulness to others.
Then only might I expect to receive.
But that would be in great measure."
Amen.

(Page. 13 Big Book)

When I am Disturbed by the Conduct (Symptoms) of Others

"This is a sick man.
How can I be helpful to him? God save me from being angry.
Thy will be done."
Amen.

(Page. 67 Big Book)

God, help me to show this person the same tolerance, pity and patience that I would cheerfully grant a sick friend.
This is a sick person, how can I be helpful to him?
God save me from being angry.
Thy will be done.
Amen.

When I am Afraid

We ask Him to remove our fear and direct our attention to what He would have us be.
God, relieve me of His fear and direct my attention to what you would have me be.
Amen.

(Page. 68 Big Book)

When I am Aware of my Own Defects and Seeking Gods Help to Change

We asked God to mould our ideals and help us to live up to them,
we ask God what we should do about each specific matter.

God, mould my ideals in this particular area of my life and help me to live up to them.
What should I do in each specific matter?
Guide me God and give me strength to do right.
Amen.
