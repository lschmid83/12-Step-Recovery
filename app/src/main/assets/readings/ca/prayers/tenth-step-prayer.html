Tenth Step Prayer

I pray I may continue: To grow in understanding and effectiveness;
To take daily spot check inventories of myself;
To correct mistakes when I make them; To take responsibility for my actions;
To be ever aware of my negative and Self - defeating attitudes and behaviours;
To keep my wilfulness in check; To always remember I need Your help;
To keep love and tolerance of others as my code; and To continue in daily prayer how I can best serve You, My Higher Power.

God, remove the Selfishness, dishonesty, resentment and fear that has cropped up in my life right now.
Help me to discuss this with someone immediately and make amends quickly if I have harmed anyone.
Help me to cease fighting anything and anyone. Show me where I may be helpful to someone else.
Help me react sanely; not cocky or afraid.
