Sixth Step Prayer

Dear God, I am ready for Your help In removing from me the defects of character.
Which I now realize are an obstacle to my recovery.
Help me to continue being honest with myself and Guide me toward spiritual and mental health.

God, help me become willing to let go of all the things to which I still cling.
Help me to be ready to let You remove all of these defects, that your will and purpose may take their place.
Amen.
